Grilled Steak and Potatoes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 steak (sirloin or ribeye, 200-250g)
  • 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: butter for topping

WHAT TO DO

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then sauté with olive oil, garlice, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Sear in a hot pan for 3-4 min per side or until done.
  4. Rest steak for 5 min.
  5. Serve with potatoes and optional butter and rosemary.
