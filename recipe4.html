Sushi

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup sushi rice (cooked)
  • 2 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • Â½ cucumber (julienned)
  • 1 small carrot (julienned)
  • 4-5 slices of raw salmon or cooked shrimp
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi (for serving)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Mix vinegar and sugar into cooked sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly over it.
  3. Add vegetables and fish in a line near the edge.
  4. Roll tightly using the mat and slice into pieces with a sharp, wet knife.
  5. Serve with soy sauce, ginger, and wasabi.
