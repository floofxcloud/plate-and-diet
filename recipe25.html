Apple Juice

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large apples (any variety)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Ice cubes (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Wash and core the apples, then chop them into chunks.
  2. Blend apples with water until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture using a fine mesh or cheesecloth.
  4. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Serve chilled with ice if desired.
