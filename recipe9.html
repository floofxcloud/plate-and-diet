Fried Chicken Sandwich

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 boneless chicken thighs or breasts
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 2 burger buns
  • Mayonnaise, pickles, lettuce (for serving)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes or overnight.
  2. Mix flour with paprika and garlic powder in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Dredge the chicken in the flour mixture, then fry until golden and cooked through.
  5. Toast the buns and spread with mayo. Add lettuce, pickles, and chicken. Assemble and enjoy.
