Mozzarella Sticks

INGREDIENTS

  • 12 mozzarella sticks (cheese)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Â½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Oil for frying
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

WHAT TO DO

  1. Dip each mozzarella stick in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.
  2. Repeat the egg and breadcrumb coating for extra crunch.
  3. Chill in the freezer for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry sticks until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve with marinara sauce.
