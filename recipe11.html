Vegetable Soup

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt, pepper, herbs to taste

WHAT TO DO

  1. In a pot, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add zucchini, green beans, and broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes until veggies are tender.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Serve warm.
