Burrito

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup guacamole or sliced avocado
  • 1/4 cup cooked ground beef or chicken
  • Shredded lettuce (optional)
  • Sour cream (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Warm the tortilla in a skillet or microwave.
  2. Layer rice, beans, meat, cheese, salsa, and avocado in the center.
  3. Add lettuce and sour cream if using.
  4. Fold in sides and roll tightly from the bottom to form a burrito.
  5. Grill seam-side down for 1-2 mins if desired. Serve warm.
