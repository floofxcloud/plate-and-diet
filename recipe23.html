Tiramisu

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 package ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Add mascarpone and mix until smooth.
  3. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form and fold into mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee (and liqueur if using) and layer in a dish.
  5. Spread half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, then dust with cocoa powder before serving.
