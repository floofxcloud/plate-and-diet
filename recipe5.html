Tacos

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 200g ground beef or chicken
  • ½ onion (diced)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ cup shredded lettuce
  • ½ cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in a pan until translucent.
  2. Add ground meat and chili powder, cook until browned.
  3. Warm tortillas on a skillet or in the microwave.
  4. Fill tortillas with meat, lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream and salsa if desired. Serve warm.
