Vegetable Stir Fry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp ginger (grated)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add all vegetables and stir-fry for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Drizzle with soy sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
