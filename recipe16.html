Granola Bars

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • ½ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts
  • ¼ cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

WHAT TO DO

  1. In a saucepan, warm peanut butter and honey until melted.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and salt.
  3. Mix oats, nuts, and chocolate chips in a bowl.
  4. Pour in the peanut butter mixture and combine well.
  5. Press into a lined pan and chill until firm. Slice and serve.
