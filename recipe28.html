Veggie Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

WHAT TO DO

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles and set aside.
  3. Sauté veggies in olive oil until tender.
  4. Layer noodles, ricotta, veggies, sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers, finish with cheese on top, and bake for 30–35 minutes.
