Cheeseburger

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 beef patty (4 oz)
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 pickle slices
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise

WHAT TO DO

  1. Grill or pan-fry the beef patty until fully cooked.
  2. Place cheese slice on top and let it melt slightly.
  3. Lightly toast the bun.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun and ketchup and mustard on the top bun.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, pickles, and the cheesy beef patty.
  6. Assemble and serve warm.
