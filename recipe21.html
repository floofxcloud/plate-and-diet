Ratatouille

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or thyme for garnish

WHAT TO DO

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until soft.
  2. Add the bell pepper, eggplant, and zucchini. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer on low for 20 minutes until veggies are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve warm.
