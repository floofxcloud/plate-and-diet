Iced Mocha Latte

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup milk (any kind)
  • Â½ cup brewed espresso or strong coffee
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in the espresso and chocolate syrup.
  3. Add milk and stir until combined.
  4. Top with whipped cream if desired. Serve chilled.
