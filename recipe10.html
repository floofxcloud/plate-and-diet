Deviled Eggs

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • Â½ tsp white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika for garnish

WHAT TO DO

  1. Boil eggs, then cool, peel, and slice in half.
  2. Remove yolks and mix them with mayo, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spoon or pipe mixture back into egg whites.
  4. Sprinkle with paprika and chill before serving.
