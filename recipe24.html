Tomato Soup

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

WHAT TO DO

  1. In a pot, heat olive oil and saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Blend the soup until smooth. Add sugar, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
