Strawberry Chiffon Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 2 cups cake flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup pureed strawberries
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cream of tartar
  • Fresh strawberries & whipped cream (for garnish)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar (half), baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add egg yolks, oil, strawberry puree, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites with cream of tartar until foamy, then gradually add the rest of the sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold egg whites into the batter until well incorporated.
  6. Pour into an ungreased chiffon or tube pan. Bake for 50–55 minutes.
  7. Invert and let cool. Top with whipped cream and fresh strawberries.
