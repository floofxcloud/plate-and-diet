Chicken Nuggets

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 chicken breasts (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs or panko
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

WHAT TO DO

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  2. Coat the chicken in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry the nuggets in batches until golden brown and cooked through (about 4-5 minutes per side).
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with dipping sauce.
