Pretzels

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup baking soda
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Coarse salt for topping

WHAT TO DO

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, salt, and sugar. Let sit 5 mins.
  2. Gradually add flour to form a dough. Knead and let rise 1 hour.
  3. Divide, shape into pretzels. Dip each into baking soda solution.
  4. Brush with egg, sprinkle salt. Bake at 450°F (230°C) for 12–15 mins.
