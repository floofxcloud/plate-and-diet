Shrimp Garden Salad

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup cooked shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 1 handful lettuce (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • ½ cucumber (sliced)
  • ¼ red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

WHAT TO DO

  1. In a large bowl, add lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Add shrimp on top and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss lightly to mix and serve chilled.
