Grilled Cheese Sandwich

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices cheddar or American cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

WHAT TO DO

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a skillet and cook sandwich until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted.
  4. Slice and serve hot.
