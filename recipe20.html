Popcorn

INGREDIENTS

  • Â¼ cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: melted butter or seasoning of choice

WHAT TO DO

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  2. Add a few kernels and cover with lid.
  3. When they pop, add the rest of the kernels and cover.
  4. Shake the pan occasionally until popping slows.
  5. Remove from heat and season with salt or toppings.
  6. Serve immediately for best crunch.
