Spaghetti

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Toss pasta with sauce and top with parmesan.
