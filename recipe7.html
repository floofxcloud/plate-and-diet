Pepperoni Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pizza dough (12-inch)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-25 slices of pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella and layer pepperoni slices.
  5. Sprinkle oregano and garlic powder on top.
  6. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
