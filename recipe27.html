Veggie Burger

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 veggie patty (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 whole wheat burger bun
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices red onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or vegan spread
  • 1 slice cheese (optional)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Cook veggie patty according to package or recipe instructions.
  2. Toast the bun lightly.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on the bun.
  4. Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, patty, and cheese.
  5. Cover with top bun and serve warm.
