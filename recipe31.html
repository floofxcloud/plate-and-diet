Macarons

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Buttercream or ganache filling

WHAT TO DO

  1. Sift almond flour and powdered sugar together.
  2. Beat egg whites until foamy, then add granulated sugar and beat to stiff peaks.
  3. Fold in dry ingredients and coloring gently.
  4. Pipe onto baking sheet and let sit 30 mins.
  5. Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 15–18 mins. Cool and fill with buttercream or ganache.
