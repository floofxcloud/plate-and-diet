Cherry Pie

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups pitted cherries
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 package pie crust
  • 1 egg (beaten for brushing)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix cherries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla.
  3. Line a pie dish with crust, fill with cherry mixture.
  4. Top with second crust, seal edges, and brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes until golden and bubbling.
