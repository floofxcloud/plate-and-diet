Potato Chips

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or vinegar seasoning

WHAT TO DO

  1. Wash and thinly slice the potatoes using a mandoline or sharp knife.
  2. Soak slices in cold water for 30 minutes, then drain and pat dry.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Fry slices in batches until golden and crispy.
  5. Remove and drain on paper towels. Season while hot and serve.
