Samosas

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas (cooked)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring roll wrappers or dough
  • Oil for frying

WHAT TO DO

  1. Heat oil and saut√© cumin, ginger garlic paste, and spices.
  2. Add mashed potatoes and peas. Cook for 5 minutes and let it cool.
  3. Fill wrappers with mixture and fold into triangles.
  4. Deep fry until golden and crispy. Serve hot with chutney.
