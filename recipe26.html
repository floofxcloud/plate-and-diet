Ice Lemon Tea

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 black tea bags
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices (for garnish)

WHAT TO DO

  1. Steep tea bags in boiling water for 5 minutes, then remove.
  2. Stir in lemon juice and sweetener if desired.
  3. Let the tea cool, then refrigerate until chilled.
  4. Serve over ice and garnish with lemon slices.
